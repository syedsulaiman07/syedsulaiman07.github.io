About Me

I'm a final-year Electronics & Communications Engineering student at PES University, Bangalore, with a deep focus on embedded systems, control theory, and mechatronics for high-performance applications.

As Electrical Lead of Vega Racing Electric, I built a Formula Student race car's complete electrical architecture from scratch — accumulator systems, safety circuits, VCU, and real-time telemetry. The team cleared Electrical Technical Inspection at Formula Bharat in their debut year.

Currently at Systemantics India, designing PCB subsystems for collaborative robots: stepper motor drivers, force-torque sensing modules, LVDT controllers, and machine vision illumination systems.

Published researcher in indoor robot localization, achieving the lowest RMSE across 5 datasets — outperforming ORB-SLAM3, VINS-Mono, and VIW-Fusion.
